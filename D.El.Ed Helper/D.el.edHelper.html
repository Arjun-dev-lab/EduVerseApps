About D.El.Ed Helper

D.El.Ed Helper is an educational app designed to support Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) students by providing free and accessible learning resources.

Features

  • Home Section: Your starting point, with easy navigation to key resources.
  • D.El.Ed Books: Access study materials tailored for D.El.Ed courses.
  • 1-7 Books: Find textbooks and resources for grades 1 through 7.
  • About the App: Learn more about what D.El.Ed Helper offers.

Monetization

D.El.Ed Helper is supported by ads to keep the app free for everyone. This includes banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads through the Facebook Audience Network, displayed thoughtfully within the app to minimize disruptions.

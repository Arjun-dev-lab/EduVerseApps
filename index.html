About EduVerse Apps

At EduVerse Apps, we are passionate about empowering learners through accessible and high-quality educational tools. Our goal is to provide students, educators, and professionals with apps that simplify complex learning processes, making education both engaging and effective. Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a teacher looking for resources, or a lifelong learner, EduVerse Apps is here to support your educational journey.

Our Apps

Our app suite includes a range of offerings that focus on key areas of academic and professional development. Each app is designed with specific user needs in mind, ensuring that learners from different educational backgrounds can benefit from targeted content and features.

  • D.El.Ed Books: This app is dedicated to students pursuing the Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) across different regions. With E-resources available in Malayalam, Tamil, and Kannada, D.El.Ed Books simplifies access to study materials, helping future teachers prepare for their roles in elementary education.
  • B.Ed Helper: B.Ed Helper is the perfect companion for Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) students. Whether you're studying at Calicut University, Kannur University, Kerala University, or MG University, this app provides valuable E-resources, including notes, reference books, and syllabus information. It's a one-stop solution for B.Ed aspirants seeking comprehensive academic support.
  • PSC Helper: Preparing for Public Service Commission (PSC) exams can be daunting, but PSC Helper makes it easier. This app offers free educational content, including video tutorials, quizzes, and mock tests designed to help users master key subjects required for various PSC exams. By combining interactive learning and self-assessment, PSC Helper ensures users are thoroughly prepared for competitive exams.
  • MGU Helper: MGU Helper is specifically designed for students enrolled in Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU). The app offers easy access to university syllabi, notes, previous year question papers, and real-time exam results for both undergraduate and postgraduate courses. Whether you're preparing for exams or looking for academic guidance, MGU Helper provides the resources you need to succeed.
